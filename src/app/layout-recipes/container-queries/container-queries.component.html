Now in all stable modern browsers, container queries are the new best way to archieve a responsive design simply because you can ship your components with built in responsivenes that does not depends on the container's size, rather than the viewport

The following are a few examples of the most common applications of container queries

1. Hide items when they don't fit

The following nav will hide a whole bunch of links when it's size crosses 320px, all in 3 tailwind classes.
